Capability areas

Four practice areas, delivered end-to-end. Engagement and communications work standalone; the AI operations, analytics, and merchandising practices compound on top.

Engagement Content & Communications

Video production, plain-language writing, web and microsite development, social campaign assets, and multi-channel engagement materials for public consultations, community outreach, and program communications. Built for distributed audiences and accessibility requirements.

AI-Powered Content Operations

Automated content production at scale for distributed networks. We build agentic systems that generate, route, and publish content across multi-location and multi-channel environments — eliminating the manual production bottleneck that constrains most communications programs.

Digital Intelligence & Analytics

GA4 implementation, attribution modeling, executive dashboards, and decision-support analytics. We translate digital activity into operating decisions, with reporting cadences and visualizations built for non-technical leadership audiences.

Merchandising & Retail Operations

AI-driven merchandising automation, content quality auditing, and inventory communication systems for Crown corporations and Indigenous-owned retail networks with multi-location footprints.

Who we work with

Positioned for Indigenous procurement programs at every level of government, plus prime contractor partnerships.

  • Indigenous governments and affiliated organizations — including MMF, First Nations governments, Tribal Councils, and Inuit organizations
  • Federal departments and agencies under PSIB set-asides and Mandatory Indigenous Business Participation requirements
  • Manitoba provincial departments under the Indigenous Procurement Initiative (IPI)
  • Crown corporations including Manitoba Hydro (Northern Purchasing Policy), Canada Post, CBC, BDC, and EDC
  • Prime contractors requiring an Indigenous sub-contractor to satisfy Mandatory Indigenous Business Participation on federal and provincial tenders

Prime contractors: we are sub-contract ready

Gallant Signal partners with non-Indigenous prime contractors who need to meet Mandatory Indigenous Business Participation requirements on federal and provincial tenders. We provide a clean compliance path with documented ownership, directory listings, and substantive technical capability in AI, content operations, communications, and digital intelligence.

We move quickly. Capability statements, letters of intent, sub-agreements, and pricing within 48 hours of request. Real scope, real delivery, real proposal visibility — not shelf participation.
